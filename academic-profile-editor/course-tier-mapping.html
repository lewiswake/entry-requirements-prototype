Direct Entry to Second Year Profiles

Tiers 07 - 11

Note: SQA Highers are generally excluded from Direct Entry 2nd year routes
  • Tier 07 (AAA / 38): Direct entry to second year Animal Behaviour BSc.
  • Tier 08 (A*A*A / 39): Direct entry to second year Applied Mathematics, Mathematics (BSc/MMath), Pure Mathematics, and Statistics (BSc/MMath).
  • Tier 09 (AAA / 38): Direct entry to second year Astrophysics, Biochemistry, Biology BSc, Chemical Sciences, Chemistry, Chemistry with Medicinal Chemistry, Earth Sciences, Environmental Earth Sciences, Geology, Materials Chemistry, Physics, Theoretical Physics.
  • Tier 10 (AAA / 38 - Slightly different IB subject reqs): Direct entry to second year Biology MBiol, Cell Biology, Ecology and Conservation, Evolutionary Biology, Marine Biology, Molecular Biology, Zoology.
  • Tier 11 (AAA / 39): Direct entry to second year Computer Science (BSc/MSci).

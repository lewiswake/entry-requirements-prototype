Ancient History MA (Honours)

4 Years Full-time | 2026 Entry

The MA (Honours) in Ancient History provides students with the opportunity to study the history of Greece and Rome, as well as the neighbouring peoples of the Mediterranean and Near East, from the Bronze Age to Late Antiquity.

Course Overview

In your first two years, you will take modules that introduce you to the broad sweep of Greek and Roman history, focusing on the key events, figures, and societal changes that shaped the ancient Mediterranean.
